Our pastor

Meet Pastor Robert LeCount Jr.

A Jacksonville native, Pastor LeCount answered the call to ministry as a young man and has spent his life since serving this city. In 2003, after years of faithful pastoring, God birthed the vision for Disciples of Christ — a church built on love for God and love for the neighborhood around it.

He leads alongside his wife, Lady Diane, with a heart that has always been in the community. He founded the Quench the Violence Rally to stand against the loss of life in our streets, and together they stay close to the work that matters — feeding, clothing, and caring for people right where they are.

"A caring leader will build up his church, and a caring church will build up its leader."

Getting the app

Add DOC to your phone

The app installs straight from your browser — no app store, no account. Find your phone below.

iPhone & iPad

Must be done in Safari — other browsers can't add it to your home screen.

  1. Open the app in Safari (tap Open the App above).
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top corner.
  5. Open the DOC icon from your home screen — and allow notifications when asked, so you never miss what matters.

Android

Works in Chrome and most browsers.

  1. Open the app in Chrome (tap Open the App above).
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  4. Tap Install to confirm.
  5. Open the DOC icon from your home screen — and allow notifications when asked, so you never miss what matters.
