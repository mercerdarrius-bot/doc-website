You belong here — and so does your gift.

At Disciples of Christ, the church is the people, and every person carries something the family needs. However you're wired and whatever you love to do, there's a place here where you'll fit right in.

Where you can serve

Every ministry is a door into the family.

01

Music & Worship Arts

Choir, musicians, and worship leaders who set the atmosphere every Sunday. If you sing or play, there's a place for your gift.

02

Praise Dance

Worship in movement. Our dance ministry leads the congregation into a deeper place through expression and grace.

03

Hospitality & Greeters

The first face every visitor sees. Ushers and greeters make sure no one walks in feeling like a stranger.

04

Youth & Children

Pouring into the next generation — teaching, mentoring, and creating a space where kids love to be.

05

Intercessory Prayer

A team that stands in the gap, lifting up the church, the community, and every request brought before God.

06

Media & Livestream

Cameras, sound, and the online broadcast that carries the Word to everyone who can't be in the room.

07

Community Outreach

Feeding, clothing, and the Quench the Violence work — taking the love of Christ into the streets of Jacksonville.
